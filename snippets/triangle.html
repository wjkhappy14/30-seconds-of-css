Creates a triangular shape with pure CSS. - Use three borders to create a triangle shape. - All borders should have the same `border-width` (`20px`). - The opposite side of where the triangle points towards (i.e. top if the triangle points downwards) should have the desired `border-color`, whereas the adjacent borders (i.e. left and right) should have a `border-color` of `transparent`. - Altering the `border-width` values will change the proportions of the triangle.
